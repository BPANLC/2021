What is BPA?

Business Professionals of America is the premier CTSO (Career and Technical Student Organization) for students pursuing careers in business management, information technology, finance, office administration and other related career fields. BPA is a co-curricular organization that supports business and information technology educators by offering co-curricular exercises based on national standards.

What is NLC?

National Leadership Conference is BPA's annual conference held in the spring. The conference brings together thousands of students from across the country to compete, showcase business skills and develop leadership. NLC is the culmination of the BPA year filled with hard work and dedication put into competitions, Torch Awards, leadership development, service and more.

Dress Code

In order to promote a professional atmosphere, students, advisors, and chaperones must follow the dress code. It is recommended that all guests follow the established dress code policy. Only members dressed in proper attire will be allowed to participate in sessions, competitive events, and on stage. Members refused entrance to an event will be given the opportunity to change into appropriate attire immediately and participate/compete in the event. Having changed into appropriate attire, the member will join testing in progress with no additional time given for missed time. Members also have the right to grieve the issue immediately after being refused entrance. Grievance of the Dress Code policy will not be accepted if not received in writing at Competitive Events headquarters within 15 minutes of the infraction in question.


Professional

Required for the following events: General Sessions, all competitive events (judged, non-judged & open), Campaign Rally, Caucuses, Workshops, National Leadership Academy:

  • Dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie
  • Pant or skirt suit
  • Suit, dress shirt, and tie
  • Dress skirt or dress slacks with coordinated blouse and/or sweater
  • Sport coat, coordinated dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie
  • BPA blazer with dress skirt or dress slacks with blouse or sweater
  • BPA blazer with dress slacks, dress shirt, and tie

**Dress shoes with dress socks or dress sandals with heel are required

**Skirt length may be no greater than two inches above the top of the knee

**This excludes all denim for any of the above

Not Accepted

The following are deemed unacceptable during BPA activities:

  • trapless, spaghetti straps, tube tops, halter tops, midriff tops
  • Spandex, lycra or transparent clothing
  • Cut-offs or ragged clothing
  • Clothing with inappropriate words and pictures
  • Swim suits must be covered with appropriate attire while in any area except the swimming pool or designated sunbathing area.

Casual Dress Code

Required for the following events: Tours, Special Events and Exhibit Hall:

  • Sportswear (jeans), pants, and shirt, t-shirt, and shorts

**Footwear is required with all of the above
